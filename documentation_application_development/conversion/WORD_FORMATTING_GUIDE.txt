WORD FORMATTING GUIDE
CS6004ES APPLICATION DEVELOPMENT COURSEWORK 2

Page setup:
- A4 page size
- Margins: 1 inch or lecturer-required margin
- Font: Times New Roman
- Body text: 12 pt
- Line spacing: 1.5
- Paragraph spacing after: 6 pt or 8 pt
- Body text justified

Headings:
- Heading 1: 16 pt, bold, black
- Heading 2: 14 pt, bold, black
- Heading 3: 12 pt, bold, black
- All headings black

Tables:
- Table text: 9 or 10 pt if needed
- Table captions above tables
- Table captions centred
- Table captions 10 pt italic

Figures:
- Figure captions below images
- Figure captions centred
- Figure captions 10 pt italic
- Export Mermaid diagrams as PNG or PDF before insertion

Final checks:
- No em dashes
- No strange symbols
- UK English
- Individual submission wording only
- Update Table of Contents in Word before PDF export
- Update List of Figures
- Update List of Tables
- Check all placeholders
- Export final PDF
- Visually inspect PDF pages
